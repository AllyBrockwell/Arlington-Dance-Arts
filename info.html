Mission Statement

Dance Arts, Inc. offers people of all ages a place to celebrate the art of dance and music. We encourage self expression while building confidence, grace, poise, and self-reliance. As we strive to promote individual growth, we also emphasize the benefits of group achievement. Fine Arts training activates the mind and body as one, as well as providing an increased appreciation for all types of music and movement.Classes provide an environment to work artistically with other,> stimulating the mind and body as one.

We are pleased you will be joining us this season. We look forward to a sensational year!
