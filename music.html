Outline of Music Programs

Voice Lessons (8 years old and up):
Private voice lessons are centered on teaching and improving a students vocal capabilities.
We will work on developing proper breathing techniques and increasing lung capacity as well as
scales, pitch, and range. With these private lessons, students will gain the technical singing abilities
and the confidence needed to perform solo or in a group setting. Voice lessons will be taped for practice
use at home and in the car. Voice lessons may be 30 minute, 45 minute, or 1 hour lessons.


Piano Lessons (5 years old and up):
Piano lessons are given on a private basis. Beginners of all ages will be taught the basics of the piano: notes, range, scales, hand placement, and correct posture while at the instrument. Private lessons will also be offered to the experienced players who are interested in improving their technique and presentation skills. Practice outlines will be set, and taped lessons will aid in improvement of skills. Piano lessons may be 30 minutes, 45 minute, or 1 hour lessons.


Guitar (7 years old and up):
Private lessons given for beginners of any age through advanced placements. Lessons may be 30 minutes, 45 minutes, or 1 hour long.


Saxophone, Flute, Clarinet (8 years old and up):
Private lessons for beginners, to band members wishing to advance a chair. Study proper breathing techniques, increase lung capacity, increase performance confidence.
