  • Please remember: tuition is not a monthly fee paid for classes, but a yearly tuition fee divided into equal parts.

Additional Fees:
  • Registration Fee: $30.00 non-refundable registration fee is due each year for the first student per family. $40.00 for a family registration, unlimited participants.
  • Costume Deposit: $50.00 costume deposit per class per dancer is due October 1st.
    • Costume deposits are not refundable.
    • Any withdraw after January 1 will incur costume balance.
    • Please be aware that costumes are not custom fit and may require alterations at your expense.
  • Costume Balances: Costume balances are due March 1st and costumes will be handed out in April.
    • This amount will be the cost of the costume minus the deposit paid.
    • The amount depends on costume selection, accessories, etc. costume balances averages $30.00-35.00.
  • Ballet Costume Rental: $30.00
    • Rental fee is due May 1st.
    • These costumes are used for our Grande Ballet for the Annual Recital, and are turned in at the end of recital.
    • This convience allows parents not to have to purchase another $90.00-100.00 costume for the ballet.
  • Annual Dance Recital Fee: $80.00
    • Covers the production and facility rental fee, includes 4 tickets to the show.
    • $25.00 fee for every child thereafter.
    • Additional dancer fees include 2 extra tickets to the show.
  • Additional Tickets:
    • May be purchased at the studio in May.
  • Annual Music Recital Fee: $50.00
